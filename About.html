How we did it:
We have based the distribution of different species on IUCN data. Imagine stacking the distribution of all species on transperent films together and cutting out different areas of the map (based on overlap of ranges) on a sketching paper. This is how we created the polygons that appear when hovering on the image of a species. These polygons are not accurate depictions of the IUCN data, but in general represent the distribution of one or more species.
